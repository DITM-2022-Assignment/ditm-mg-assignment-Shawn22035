What Can You Do?

There are many ways for people to reduce the amount of carbon emissions they produce daily. But a few ways that you can reduce your carbon emissions are:

  • Walk To School
  • Switch Off Appliances When Unused
  • Save Water
  • Use Recycleable/ More Environmentally Friendly Things

There are many more things that you as an individual can do to reduce your carbon footprint. Even doing the smallest things to help the environment can snowball into a much healthier planet and surroundings
